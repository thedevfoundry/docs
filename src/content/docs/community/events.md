---
title: Event Formats
description: Standard formats and expectations for Dev Foundry events.
---

Dev Foundry events are designed to increase output, accountability, and collaboration.

## 1) Coworking Sessions

Purpose: create protected build time with community support.

Format:

- 10-15 minute kickoff with goals
- focused build blocks
- optional unblock check-ins
- short closeout with shipped work and next steps

Expectations:

- arrive with a clear task
- work on real project deliverables
- share what you completed before leaving

## 2) Demo Nights

Purpose: make progress visible and reward shipping.

Format:

- short demos of what members built
- practical feedback from the community
- discussion of next milestones and support needed

Expectations:

- demo real progress (prototype, release, experiment, insight)
- keep demos concise and practical
- ask for specific feedback or help

## 3) Incubation Sessions

Purpose: help ideas become validated projects and teams.

Format:

- members pitch ideas, problems, or experiments
- collaborators form around promising work
- teams define immediate next actions

Expectations:

- be open to feedback and iteration
- focus on evidence over assumptions
- leave with ownership and a short-term plan

## Event quality bar

A good Dev Foundry event produces:

- shipped work
- clear next actions
- stronger collaboration across members
